About Abu Dhabi APPROVAL

10 Years of Experience

Empowering Your Projects with Expert Approval Solutions in Abu Dhabi

At Abu Dhabi Approval Services, we are a team of seasoned professionals with extensive experience in the approval process landscape. Our diverse expertise spans civil engineering, structural design, construction management, MEP services, villa design, interior design, project management, and Authority of Record (AOR) services. With a deep understanding of local regulations and industry best practices, we are equipped to handle even the most intricate approval challenges.

About US

Who We Are:

We are a dynamic team of experts dedicated to providing comprehensive engineering solutions for projects in Abu Dhabi. With a wealth of experience and knowledge in the field, we have earned a reputation for our innovative approach and commitment to excellence.

What We Offer:

At our company, we offer a complete suite of engineering solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each project. From initial concept to final approval, we guide our clients through a smooth and hassle-free procedure, ensuring their vision becomes a reality. Our expertise covers a wide range of areas, including civil engineering, structural design, MEP services, and more.

Our Approach:

We understand the importance of obtaining APPROVAL from various authorities in Abu Dhabi, including DMT, ADCD, ADM, Al Ain Municipality, Al Dhafrah Municipality, ITC, DOH, Food Control and Safety, ADDC, DCT, Masdar City, KIZAD, Industrial City, and many more. With our in-depth knowledge of the approval process and strong relationships with these authorities, we streamline the process for our clients, saving them time and effort.

Why Choose Us:

Clients choose us for our unwavering commitment to accuracy, creativity, and client satisfaction. Our team's innovative thinking and attention to detail ensure that every project is executed to the highest standards, delivering exceptional results every time.
